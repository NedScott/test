Note: This keymap must be applied onto the most recent Jarvis nightly build, if you want to try it.
This does the same as the jarvis_low_button_keymap.xml map, but adds:
Fullscreen video/music:
[UP] - Volume up
[DOWN] - Volume down
No longpress UP/DOWN actions to allow for key repeat
Live TV/radio:
[UP] - Volume up
[DOWN] - Volume down
No longpress UP/DOWN actions to allow for key repeat
[LEFT] - Channel down
[RIGHT] - Channel up
